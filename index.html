Blockchain Technology

Blockchain technology is utilized to maintain a tamper-proof and transparent record of all interactions with the stored medical images. Each time an image is uploaded or accessed, a unique cryptographic hash value is generated and stored on the blockchain ledger.With blockchain technology, healthcare professionals can trust that the medical images they are viewing have not been altered since their initial upload, enhancing data security and privacy.

Cloud Infrastructure

Our healthcare organization leverages cloud infrastructure to securely store and manage encrypted medical image.By utilizing cloud services, we ensure high availability, reliability, and accessibility of medical images to authorized healthcare professionals from any location and at any time.Additionally, cloud infrastructure enhances data backup and disaster recovery capabilities, ensuring the continuity of healthcare services even in the face of unforeseen events
